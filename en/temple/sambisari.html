Sambisari Temple

Sambisari temple is a Hindu temple built in the 9th century AD in the days of Ancient Mataram Kingdom, during the reign of Rakai Garung. Sambisari position located 6.5 meters below ground level, most likely due to buried lava from Mount Merapi, which erupted on a large scale in the early 11th century (probably in 1006). This is evident from the number of volcanic rock material around the temple. It is estimated that the total area of ​​the site is much larger than what can be seen now. However, archaeologists say that the temple could not be explored because of the height of land. It is feared that if they dig further temple will be flooded by water from the river in the west during the rainy season.

After being buried for hundreds of years, the first chunk of the great cultural heritage of the past was discovered in 1966 It took 21 years to excavate and assemble hundreds of stone before finally Sambisari successfully reconstructed. The temple complex consists of 1 parent and 3 temples temples companion (Perwara). In the middle there Perwara Padmasana statue that may be used to put offerings. The main temple building is quite unique because it does not have a pedestal like other temples in Java. Foot of the temple also functions as a pedestal so parallel to the ground. The foot of the temple left plain, with no reliefs or decorations. Various ornaments are generally in the form of new simbar found at the top of the body to the outside of the temple. Garnish it glance like batik motifs. On the outside wall on the north side of the main building there is a niche containing a statue of Durga Mahesasuramardini, east there is a statue of Ganesha, the statue of Agastya to the south, and to the west there are two statues of gods doorman, Mahakala and Nandiswara. In the main temple there is phallus and yoni with a large enough size & mdash; indicates that this temple was built as a place of worship of Lord Shiva. At the time of excavation discovered a variety of other objects in them are some of the pottery, jewelry, metal mirror, as well as the inscription.

Candi Sambisari dapat diakses dengan kendaraan umum. Akses menuju candi ini sangat mudah, dan untuk menuju lokasi candi kita dapat menggunakan kendaraan roda dua maupun kendaraan roda empat karena akses jalan dan lokasi parkir yang luas. Kita bisa menjangkau dengan berkendara melewati lintas jalan Yogyakarta-Solo Km 10 (perhatikan tanda jalan) hingga menemukan papan penunjuk menuju candi ini. Ketika melintasi jalan raya Yogyakarta-Solo, begitu sampai tepat di depan gerbang kompleks Akademi Angkatan Udara, berbeloklah ke kiri memasuki jalan kecil beraspal halus. Selanjutnya kita tinggal berbelok ke kiri mengikuti alur jalan kecil tersebut sampai ujung karena Candi Sambisari berada di ujung jalan itu.
