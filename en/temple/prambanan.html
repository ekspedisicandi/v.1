Prambanan is the largest Hindu temple in Indonesia. Built around 850 BC by the Sanjaya dynasty. Prambanan is a Hindu civilization in the land of Java. This can be seen in the temple structure that describes the core belief in Hinduism (Trimurti). The specialty is the beauty of Prambanan reliefs on the walls of the temple, the main story of the Ramayana into reliefs at Prambanan. Other relief that is not less interesting is Kalpataru tree that the Hindu religion is considered as a tree preservation, environmental compatibility and life. Relief of Kalpataru tree is described middle flanking a lion, describes the Java community to the 9th century had wisdom to manage their environment.

Prambanan temple is often referred to as Rara Jonggrang. It is associated with the legend that is believed by some people about the history of Prambanan temple Java.
