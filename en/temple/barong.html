Barong temple is a Hindu temple have been built in the 9th century and into the 10th, as the relics of the kingdom of Mataram Medang period. Barong serves as a place of worship of Lord Vishnu and his wife Lakshmi, also known as Dewi Sri (Goddess of prosperity agriculture). Lord Vishnu and Goddess Sri desembah in the hope that they will be more fertile ground. Read the information boards of Barong, the worship of Lord Vishnu and Goddess Sri these, probably caused by soil conditions around the temple barren and infertile, so by worshiping Lord Vishnu and Goddess Sri expected to be fertile soil conditions.

Unlike the other temples in Central Java, Barong is punden staircase buildings, namely the model of sacred buildings in the prahindu. Barong temple complex has an entrance on the west, and ushered in a three terraced land. Judging from the location of the stairs up from terrace to terrace, this Hindu temple faces west. The first and second terraces have not found the temple, although there are remnants of the floor. The second terrace is fairly wide open area. Before entering the highest terrace there is a small gate paduraksa flanking the stairs up. On the highest terrace of the building there are two temples to worship, is expected to Lord Vishnu and Goddess Sri.

There is no relief decoration on the wall and the foot of the building, there are only patterned foliage and sculptured human figure simple. Stackable temple roof with a pointed peak.

Barong is not accessible by public transport. From Yogyakarta up TransJogja 1A or 1B lane and stop at Prambanan shelter. Continue with ojek (motorcycle taxi) to the temple. Barong Temple can be reached by car about 16 minutes from Prambanan, or about 1 hour 44 minutes by foot barjalan. Or by private vehicle, departing from Yogyakarta to Solo Street or take the route to Prambanan. Turn right at the intersection before the Prambanan Temple (south) and follow the road sign to the Barong and Banyunibo Temple. Barong Temple can be reached by car about 30 minutes from Yogyakarta. Ask for help if you get lost population.
