Sambisari Temple



Sambisari temple built in the 10th century around around 812-838 AD. It was built by the Sailendra dynasty during the reign of Rakai Garung precisely. This temple was accidentally discovered by a farmer in 1966 after buried by volcanic material from the eruption of Merapi in 1006.
