Kedulan Temple



Kedulan temple is a Hindu temple built in the 8th century and 9th during the days of Ancient Mataram Kingdom. The temple is three to seven meters below ground level because buried in lava from Mount Merapi which erupted on a large scale in the early 11th century (circa 1006).
