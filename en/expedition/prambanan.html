Prambanan Temple



Prambanan is the largest Hindu temple in Indonesia, which was built in the 9th century AD. This temple is dedicated to Trimurti (three main Hindu gods) of Brahma (God of creation), Vishnu (God the Preserver), and Shiva (God of Destruction).
